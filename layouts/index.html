What is Invention Lab?

Invention Lab 2026 is a 5-day hands-on training program for Sea Cadets that introduces the fundamentals of embedded systems, sensors, 3D printing, IoT communication, and project-based capstone work. Each day builds on the last, guiding students from their first lines of firmware code to a fully integrated demo-ready project.
