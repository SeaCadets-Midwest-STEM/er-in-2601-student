This section contains the detailed lab manuals for each session of Invention Lab 2026. Each lab manual provides step-by-step instructions, safety guidelines, parts lists, code examples, and troubleshooting guides.

## How to Use These Manuals

Each lab manual is organized by day and session. Before starting a lab:

1. **Read the Safety section** - always review safety guidelines first
2. **Check your Parts List** - verify all components are present
3. **Follow the Procedure** - work through each step with your team
4. **Complete the Verification Checklist** - confirm everything works
5. **Try Challenge Extensions** - if you finish early
