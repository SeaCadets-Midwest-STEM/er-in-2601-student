## Theme: Capstone Project Build, Demo Day Presentations, and Career Pathways

On Day 5, your team brings everything together. Working at your Creation Station, you'll design, build, and present an integrated capstone project that combines firmware, sensors, physical design, and connectivity.

### Sessions

- **"The Final Countdown"** - Capstone Kickoff and Challenge Reveal
- **Team Project Scoping** - Turn an idea into a buildable plan using all components at your station
- **Build Block I** - Rapid prototyping: design, build, test, iterate
- **Build Block II** - Integration: combine firmware, sensors, 3D-printed parts, and MQTT
- **Demo Prep** - Practice your presentation and polish your project
- **Demo Day** - Live presentations to instructors and peers
- **Career Pathways** - Where to go from here in embedded systems, robotics, and IoT engineering

### Capstone Challenge

Teams will choose from a set of challenge prompts provided by instructors or propose their own (with instructor approval). Your project should combine concepts from multiple days of training.

### Available Components at Your Station

By Day 5, your Creation Station has everything accumulated over the week:

- Arduino Uno Q with all sketches from Days 1-4
- Breadboard with wired circuits (LEDs, sensors, buttons)
- 2004 I2C LCD display for real-time output
- HC-SR04 ultrasonic sensor, PIR sensor, TMP36 temperature sensor
- SG90 servo, DC motor with driver
- microSD card for data logging
- Buzzer, mini breadboard, 3D-printed parts from Day 3
- MQTT connectivity to the local broker

### Demo Day Evaluation

In the afternoon, each team presents a live demo. Evaluations focus on:

| Criteria | What We Look For |
|----------|-----------------|
| Functionality | Does it work as described? |
| Integration | Does it combine concepts from multiple days? |
| Creativity | Is the approach original and well thought out? |
| Presentation | Can the team clearly explain their design and decisions? |

### Key Takeaways

- You've written firmware, wired circuits, designed 3D models, and connected devices over a network.
- You've experienced the full product development cycle from concept to demo.
- You've built a portfolio-ready project to share with schools, mentors, and future teams.

### What to Bring

- Your team - all components, circuits, and 3D prints are at your Creation Station
- Energy and creativity - it's build day!
